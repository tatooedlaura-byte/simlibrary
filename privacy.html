Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

SimLibrary ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

SimLibrary is a single-player offline game. All game data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game progress and save data
  • Game settings and preferences
  • Achievement progress

This data is stored using your device's local storage and never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

SimLibrary does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

SimLibrary is designed to be family-friendly and safe for users of all ages. We do not collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all game data by:

  • Using the "Restart Tower" option in the game's Stats menu
  • Uninstalling the app from your device
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
