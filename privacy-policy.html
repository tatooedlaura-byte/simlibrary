SimLibrary Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

SimLibrary is a library-builder idle game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. SimLibrary does not require an account, login, or any personal information to play.

Local Storage

Your game progress is saved locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Analytics

SimLibrary does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Third-Party Services

SimLibrary does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

SimLibrary is designed to be family-friendly and safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub page.
