Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hire staff?

Staff applicants appear in your lobby (ground floor). Tap on them to see their info and hire them to a floor. Try to match them with their "dream floor" for a 2x restock speed bonus!

What are VIPs?

VIP visitors (famous authors, celebrities) appear in the lobby with a golden glow. Tap to pick them up, then tap a floor to drop them off and receive special bonuses like instant restocks or star bursts.

How do I earn more stars?

Keep your books stocked! Readers check out books and earn you stars. Hire staff to unlock more book categories, and upgrade floors to increase capacity.

What are Tower Bucks for?

Tower Bucks (diamonds) can be used to instantly restock all floors, rush construction, and purchase special upgrades.

My progress was lost!

Game progress is saved locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be lost. We recommend using the Export feature in Settings to back up your save.
